Product Comparison

Check products in the left panel to add them to the comparison table. Use the Product Category dropdown to filter the list.

Package Builder

Combine products, set budget, and see your blended CPM

Select products from the left panel to add them to your package. Use the search box or category filter to find products quickly.
Enter a Total Package Budget and click Distribute Evenly to split it equally across all products — or enter individual budgets for each line item manually.
The Blended CPM at the bottom updates live, showing the overall average CPM across your entire package weighted by spend.
If your package includes Amazon or Weather Co. products, an Add-ons panel will appear allowing you to apply optional CPM add-ons that adjust affected product rates automatically.
An amber warning will appear on any line item where the allocated budget falls below that product's monthly minimum spend.

Campaign Impact

Estimate reach & frequency for a campaign

Pre-Campaign Planning — enter your audience size, impressions, and duration to forecast reach, frequency, and overall market impact before you launch.
Industry benchmark for effective digital frequency is 3–7 exposures per person. Below 3 is under-impact; above 7 risks audience fatigue.
Estimation notice: Reach and frequency figures are statistical estimates based on industry-standard models and assume random impression distribution. Actual campaign results will vary based on targeting, platform delivery, and audience behavior. These tools are intended for planning purposes only and should not be used as guarantees of performance.
